Voice information

The app first looks for an Indian female English voice and a Telugu voice installed in Chrome or Edge. When a Telugu system voice is unavailable, it uses an online Telugu audio fallback. Internet is therefore helpful for Telugu speech.

Daily routine

  1. Five words: pronounce and hear Telugu meanings.
  2. Read one PDF page with the teacher guide.
  3. Search unfamiliar words in Book Words.
  4. Write or type three words.
  5. Play one game and finish with praise.
